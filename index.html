Rafflemania

Rafflemania is an application built with Rails that allows users to enter raffles for Amazon products. Users can authenticate with a username/password, or with their Amazon account. After authentication, users may browse an assortment of items and enter the raffles with purchasable ‘tickets’. All transactions and raffle entries are stored in the database.
